Privacy Policy — PSAS

Last updated: 20/07/2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PSAS ("we", "us", "the app"), operated by Pegasus Sports PERFORMANCE PSYCHOLOGY, collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application for booking fitness classes, appointments, and managing memberships ("the App").

Information We Collect

We collect the following information to operate the App:

  • Account information — name, email address, and profile photo, obtained via Google Sign-In when you create an account.
  • Profile details — phone number, membership/role status, and credit balance, which you or our staff provide.
  • Booking activity — classes, appointments, and waiting-list entries you book, cancel, or join, including dates, times, and session history.
  • Payment records — plan purchased, amount, currency, and invoice details. We do not collect or store your card details — payments are processed directly by Stripe, our third-party payment processor.
  • Communications — booking confirmation emails and calendar invites are sent to the email address associated with your account or booking.
  • Device information — used only to deliver local notifications and reminders on your device; we do not use this for tracking or advertising.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account and bookings
  • To process membership purchases and payments
  • To send booking confirmations, calendar invites, and service-related emails
  • To allow our trainers and administrative staff to manage classes, appointments, and member records
  • To maintain the security and integrity of the App

We do not use your information for advertising, and we do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

The App relies on the following third-party services to operate:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore Database, Cloud Functions) — stores your account and booking data, and runs backend logic. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Sign-In — used to authenticate your account.
  • Stripe — processes membership payments. Stripe handles your payment card details directly; we never receive or store full card numbers. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.

Data Sharing

Your profile and booking information is visible to our trainers and administrative staff as needed to provide our services (e.g. confirming your bookings, managing your membership, or resolving support requests). We do not share your personal information with any other third party except as required to operate the services listed above, or where required by law.

Data Retention

We retain your account and booking information for as long as your account is active, or as needed to comply with our legal and accounting obligations (e.g. payment records). You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time — see "Your Rights" below.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures, including encrypted connections and access-controlled databases, to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
